## Titlebar

The _**Titlebar**_  offers quick shortcuts to the _**Save**_ the document, _**Save a Copy**_, _**Import Photo**_, _**Preferences**_, undo, redo and delete commands. 

(video: titlebar1-iphone.mp4 width: 750)

On the right side are four icons which open sliding panes with additional features. 
Each pane has a distinct role, from changing various object settings to saving and reusing styles and object libraries.

(video: titlebar2-iphone.mp4 width: 750)
