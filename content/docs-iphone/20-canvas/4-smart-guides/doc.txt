# Smart Guides

Smart guides are straight, dotted lines (vertical or horizontal) that show up whenever you create or move an object, making it easier to align text or objects.

To enable or disable this feature, open the _**Grid pane**_ from the Properties panel and tap the _**Smart Guides**_ option.
