# Polygons

Choose the _**Polygon tool**_ to create polygons with various numbers of sides, including triangles, hexagons, octagons, etc.. Tap and drag to create a polygon. 

(video: polygons1-iphone.mp4 width: 750)

Vary the number of sides of your polygon by adjusting the _**Sides**_ setting in the _**Tool pane**_.
