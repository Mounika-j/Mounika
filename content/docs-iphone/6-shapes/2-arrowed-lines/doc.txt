# Arrowed Lines

Choose the _**Arrowed Line tool**_ to draw arrowed lines. Tap and drag to create an arrowed line. 

As with the Line tool, tap and drag to begin drawing an arrowed line, and then tap and hold with a second finger to easily create horizontal, vertical or 45Â° arrowed lines.
