# Dimension Lines

Choose the _**Dimension Line tool**_ to draw dimension lines. Simply tap and drag to create a dimension line. 

As with the other line tools, tap and drag to begin drawing a dimension line, and then tap and hold with a second finger to easily create horizontal, vertical or 45Â° dimension lines.

(video: dimensionlines1-iphone.mp4 width: 750)

The line endings and the line dash styles for created dimension lines can be customized using the (link: docs-iphone/effects/strokes#stroke-pane text: _**Stroke pane **_) or the (link: docs-iphone/effects/strokes text: _**Effects pane**_)  from the Appearance panel. 

The position and style of the dimension labels can be customized using the (link: docs-iphone/labels/adding-labels text: _**Label pane**_) from the Appearance panel. 

(video: dimensionlines2-iphone.mp4 width: 750)

Create intricate drawings, such as floor plans or architectural designs, using lines, arrowed lines and dimension lines.
