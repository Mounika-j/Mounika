### Snap to Grid

Open the _**Grid pane**_ from the Properties panel and enable the _**Snap to Grid**_ setting to snap objects and points to the nearest grid position when creating or moving them.

### Snap to Point

Open the _**Grid pane**_ from the Properties panel and enable the _**Snap to Point**_ setting  to snap points to the nearest anchor point position when moving them.
