#Ordering Documents

Select between ordering existing documents by name or by date. To view this control, swipe down from the top of the documents list.

(video: sortingdocuments1-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

#Renaming Documents

To rename a document, simply tap on its name label in the _**Documents Browser**_.
