# Rounded Rectangles

Choose the _**Rounded Rectangle tool**_ to create rectangles or squares with rounded corners. Tap and drag to create a rectangle with rounded corners.

Tap and drag to begin drawing the rounded rectangle, and then tap and hold with a second finger to turn the drawn shape into a square with rounded corners.

Vary the roundness of the corners by adjusting the _**Radius**_ setting in the _**Tool pane**_.
