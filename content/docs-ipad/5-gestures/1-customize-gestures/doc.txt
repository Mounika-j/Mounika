#Customize Gestures

You can set the _**Two-finger Tap**_, _**Three-finger Tap**_ and _**Four-finger Tap**_ gestures to perform one of the following commands: switch to the previously used tool, activate the Eraser tool, open the Color panel, Undo or Redo the last command.

(video: customizegestures1-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

Tap the cogwheel button from the _**Titlebar**_ to open the _**Preferences panel**_, and then tap the _**Customize Gestures**_ button.
