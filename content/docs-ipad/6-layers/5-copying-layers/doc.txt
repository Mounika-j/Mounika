#Copying Layers


Layers can be copied from one document and pasted inside another document. 

To copy a layer, open the _**Layers Action menu**_ and then choose _**Copy Layer**_. To paste a layer, open the _**Layers Action menu**_ and then choose _**Paste Layer**_.
