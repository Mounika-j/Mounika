#Merging Layers

The contents of two layers can be merged into one single layer. To merge two layers, select the layer on top, open the _**Layers Action menu**_ and then choose _**Merge Down**_.

To merge all visible layers from the Layers panel, open the _**Layers Action menu**_ and then choose _**Merge Visible**_.
