# Aligning Objects

Use the _**Align**_ commands from the _**Arrange pane**_ of the Properties panel to align the selected objectsâ€™ positions relative to each other or the borders of the canvas. Using one of the six commands you can align objects as follows:

**Align Left** - Aligns selected objects to the left edge of the leftmost selected object on your canvas. 

(video: aligningobjects1-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

**Align Right** - Aligns selected objects to the right edge of the rightmost selected object on your canvas. 

(video: aligningobjects2-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

**Align Top** - Aligns selected objects to the top edge of the uppermost selected object on your canvas. 

(video: aligningobjects3-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

**Align Bottom** - Aligns selected objects to the bottom edge of the lowermost selected object on your canvas. 

(video: aligningobjects4-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

**Align Horizontal** - Aligns selected objects in a row according to the center of the uppermost selected object on your canvas. 

(video: aligningobjects5-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

**Align Vertical** - Aligns selected objects in a column according to the center of the leftmost selected object on your canvas.

(video: aligningobjects6-ipad.mp4 width: 750)

Having only one object/group selected when tapping an _**Align**_ command will align the selection according to the edges of the canvas. 

(video: aligningobjects7-ipad.mp4 width: 750)
