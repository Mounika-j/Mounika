# Pattern Palette

Using the tab menu at the top of the _**Color panel**_, you can easily switch to the _**Pattern Palette**_. 

Patterns can be added to the _**Pattern Palette**_ by clicking on the add button (**+**) at the bottom of the palette. Saved patterns remain stored inside the _**Pattern Palette**_ and can be used in any new designs until you choose to remove them. 

To remove a pattern simply right-click on it and choose _**Remove Pattern**_.
