# Deleting Layers

To delete the currently selected layer, click the _**Delete Layer**_ button (**−**), or choose _**Delete Layer**_ from the _**Layer actions menu**_. 

Alternatively, you can choose **Arrange > Delete Layer** [**⌃** - **⌘** - **−**] from the menu bar, or simply hit the **Delete**/**Backspace** key. This will remove the layer and all of its objects. 

(video: deletinglayers1.mp4  width: 750)


> Tip: You can also access the **Layer actions menu** by **right-clicking** on a layer.
