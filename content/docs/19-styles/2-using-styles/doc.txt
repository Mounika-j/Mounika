# Copy/Paste Styles

Besides the _**Styles panel**_, appearance attributes can be easily passed from one object to another using quick menu commands. 

Choose **Edit > Copy Style** [**⌥** - **⌘** - **C**] from the menu bar to copy the style of a selected object and **Edit > Paste Style** [**⌥** - **⌘** - **V**] to paste a copied style to any other object. 
Alternatively, you can simply right click on the canvas and go to **Copy Style** or **Paste Style**.
