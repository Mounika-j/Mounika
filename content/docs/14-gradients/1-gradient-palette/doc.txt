# Gradient Palette

Use the tab menu at the top of the _**Color panel**_, you can easily switch to the _**Gradient Palette**_. 

Gradients can be added to the _**Gradient Palette**_ by clicking on the add button (**+**) at the bottom of the palette. Saved gradients remain stored inside the _**Gradient Palette**_ and can be used in any new designs until you choose to remove them. 

To remove a gradient simply right-click on it and choose _**Remove Gradient**_.

(image: gradientpalette1.jpg  width: 750)

Create new gradients using the gradient editor in the (link: docs/gradients/customizing-gradients text: _**Fill pane**_) or the (link: docs/effects/fills text: _**Effects pane**_) from the _**Appearance panel**_.
