Text: 

### Placing Text on Path

To place a text on a path, select your text and the path on which you wish to place your text and simply go to **Modify > Place Text on Path**.

(video: textonapath1.mp4 width: 750)

### Adjusting Text on Path

Use the round handle to move your text on the path. Text on a path is fully editable. Simply double-click it to change its content or its attributes. 

(video: textonapath2.mp4 width: 750)

Go to _**Modify > Reverse Path Direction**_ to quickly flip your text on the other side of the path. 

(video: textonapath3.mp4 width: 750)

### Releasing Text from Path

Select your text on a path and go to _**Modify > Release Text from Path**_ to easily separate your text from the path.

(video: textonapath4.mp4 width: 750)
