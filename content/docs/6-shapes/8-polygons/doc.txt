# Polygons

Choose the _**Polygon tool**_ to draw polygons with various numbers of sides, including triangles, hexagons, octagons, etc. Simply click and drag inside your Canvas to create a new shape.

Vary the number of sides by adjusting the _**Sides**_ setting in the _**Tool Options bar**_.

(video: polygons1.mp4 width: 750)

> Tip: Use the **Shift - O** keyboard shortcut to quickly select the **Polygon tool**.
