# Stars

Choose the _**Star tool**_ to draw star shapes. You can create simple star shapes or more complex objects with numerous rays and long sides.

Vary the number of points and the ray length, using the _**Points**_ and the _**Edge Length**_ settings in the _**Tool Options bar**_.

(video: stars1.mp4 width: 750)

> Tip: Use the **J** keyboard shortcut to quickly select the **Star tool**.
